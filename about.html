ABOUT US

ISHWAR GROUP company was founded in 1983 with Custom House Agent division and that is how we began our footprints in Logistics Trade. Since last 38 years we have grown in different division which you can have a look on in the upcoming slides. Our aim is to ensure a hassle free services to our client.

We are an independent private company offering customised logistics and transport solutions worldwide. Our Head Office is in Gurgaon. Simultaneously, we do own registered offices at all major Sea ports &ICD's with working staff of 500+ across India. We are a trusted partner of our clients, operating within many sectors and transporting around the globe.
